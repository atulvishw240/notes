
## Cyber Crime and Criminal Justice

## 1. What is Cyber Crime?

**Cyber crime** refers to **illegal activities committed using computers, networks, or digital systems**, where:

- Computer can be **target**
    
- Computer can be **tool**
    
- Computer can be **both**
    

### Definition (writeable):

> Cyber crime is any unlawful act where a computer, network, or digital system is used as a tool, target, or place of crime.

## 5. Cyber Law & Criminal Justice System

### What is Cyber Law?

Cyber law deals with **legal issues related to the use of computers, networks, and the internet**.

It defines:

- What is a cyber crime
    
- Punishment
    
- Investigation process
    
- Evidence handling
