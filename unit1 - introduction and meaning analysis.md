
## Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this unit, you will be able to:

- discuss the concept of critical thinking and its importance;
- distinguish between the literal and implied meanings of sentences;
- explain concepts of necessary and sufficient conditions and examples of each;
- analyze statements for various types of obscurity, such as lexical ambiguity, referential ambiguity, syntactic ambiguity, vagueness, incomplete, and meaning;
- describe the criteria for evaluating definitions and sources;
- apply criteria for evaluating definitions and sources to real-world scenarios.
